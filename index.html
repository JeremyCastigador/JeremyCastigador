About

I am Jeremy Karl M. Castigador, a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at Bulacan State University with a strong focus on Web and Mobile Application Development.

I am a dedicated and detail-oriented full-stack developer, proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java, with a solid foundation in building functional, user-centered applications. Driven by a passion for coding and problem-solving, I continuously seeks to improve my technical skills and create efficient digital solutions.

One of my notable projects, "JKC (Jeremy Karl Computershop)," is a system designed to streamline the management of customer accounts in a computer shop environment. The application helps track user sessions, monitor usage, and organize account data, enabling shop owners to operate more efficiently while providing better service to customers.

I combine technical competence with a strong willingness to learn, aiming to develop impactful systems that solve real-world problems.
